Some information that may help in getting to the conference:

Flights

The two major airports that may be of use are Dublin and Shannon. Dublin has, by far, the most connections and there are good bus services from Dublin airport to Limerick.

Skyscanner This is a good starting point when looking to see what carriers you may be able to use.

General Info

Conference and Sports BureauThis website site gives some info that may be of use, although some appears to be out of date.

Ground Transportation

Dublin CoachProvides a service from Dublin Airport to the university campus in Limerick. The route is the M7. To me, this looks like the best option.

JJ KavanaghA little more expensive, also covers this route.

Bus EireannAlso provides a coach service.

Shannon Airport Bus

Visas for Ireland

Persons travelling to Ireland may wish to check visa requirements. You can do so here. It would be a shame to book for the event and be turned back at point of entry because you did not have the correct visa or passport.
